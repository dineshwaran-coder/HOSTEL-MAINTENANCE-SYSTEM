About HostelFix Portal

HostelFix is a comprehensive, closed-loop hostel maintenance and facility operations system designed to bridge the communication gap between hostel residents, wardens, administration managers, and technical workers.

Our platform automates structural workflows: from initial complaint registration with photo attachment, through admin verification and worker dispatching, to final sign-off inspection and student feedback loops.

Key Pillars of the System:

  • Transparency: Timeline steppers show exactly where the ticket resides.
  • Speed: Auto-matching categories directly route requests to plumbers, electricians, and carpenters.
  • Data-Driven: Detailed metrics help management evaluate monthly maintenance costs and response velocities.
